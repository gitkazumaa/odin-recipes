PB & J

Description

A simple, yet tasty, peanut butter and jelly sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 2 tbps Peanut butter
  • 2 tbps Strawberry Jam

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread strawberry jam on on the other slice of bread
  3. Put the two pieces of bread togther
  4. Done!
